❓ How do others open this app?
The app is a single HTML file — no install, opens in any browser.

To create a permanent shareable link in 2 minutes:
  1. Go to netlify.com/drop
  2. Drag & drop your accord_v2.html file onto the page
  3. You instantly get a free URL like sunny-dawn-12345.netlify.app
  4. Anyone can open that URL on their phone or laptop — no download, no install

The room invite link above works once they have the app open at your hosted URL.
